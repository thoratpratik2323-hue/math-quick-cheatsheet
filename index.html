Infinite Series Trick If $y = \sqrt{f(x) + \sqrt{f(x) + \dots\infty}}$, then

Definite Power Hack
$\int_0^{\pi/2} \frac{\sqrt{\sin x}}{\sqrt{\sin x} + \sqrt{\cos x}} dx = \frac{\pi}{4}$

True for any power $n$: $(\sin x)^n / [(\sin x)^n + (\cos x)^n]$

King's Property ($P_4$) solves 80% of Definite Integrals board questions!

Matrix Mind-Benders

Determinant Factor Hack $|adj A| = |A|^{n-1}$
Zero Determinant Cases 1. Skew-Symmetric matrix of Odd Order. 2. Rotation Matrix: $\begin{bmatrix} \cos\theta & -\sin\theta \\ \sin\theta & \cos\theta \end{bmatrix}$ (Det = 1).
Shortcut for Inverse (2x2) Swap diagonals, change signs of off-diagonals, divide by $|A|$.

Vector & 3D Shortcuts

Shortest Distance Hack Distance between parallel lines:
$d = \frac{|(\vec{a}_2 - \vec{a}_1) \times \vec{b}|}{|\vec{b}|}$
Perpendicular Distance Point $(x_1, y_1, z_1)$ to plane $Ax+By+Cz+D=0$:
$\frac{|Ax_1 + By_1 + Cz_1 + D|}{\sqrt{A^2+B^2+C^2}}$
Direction Ratios $(a,b,c)$ are your best friends. Normalize them to get Direction Cosines $(l,m,n)$.
